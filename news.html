What's New?

It was an absolute privilege to speak at the 2017 International Association of Human Trafficking Investigators Conference on blockchain technologies and cryptocurrencies. You can find the presentation here. I encourage investigators to contact me with any additional questions they might have.

The Bluegrass Developers Guild website is now up! I plan to organize an inaugural meet and greet in the Lexington area. Please join the Meetup group and I will post an event soon.

After a long hiatus, I have once again started writing blogs. Check them out and let me know what you think! I am also drafting two peer-reviewed papers as well; more to follow soon.

Forging Blockchains: Spatial Production and Political Economy of Decentralized Cryptocurrency Code/Spaces, is finally published! I have posted a PDF copy of the thesis for free through GitHub. If you feel there are any issues with the content, methodology, or anything else, please post an issue within the repository. If you wish to use any of the content, please contact me as it is copyrighted.
