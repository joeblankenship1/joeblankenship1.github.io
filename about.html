About Me

Geographer, Data Geek, Developer, Engineer, Scientist, Hacker


I am the founder and lead data scientist at the Cyber Geography Research Institute International. CGRII is an all-volunteer organization which aims to explore and define cyberspace via geographic principles.

I will also be pursuing my PhD in Geography at the University of Kentucky. I plan to continue my work on cyber geographies, code/spaces, political economy, and information society.

I sponsor and organize the Bluegrass Developers Guild as I am a huge data geek who loves programming, analytics, and technology. I also teach a variety of classes (contact me for additional information).
